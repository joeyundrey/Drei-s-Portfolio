Soulful Sundays — Project
A weekly literary & arts web series — goals and outcomes
Introduction
Soulful Sundays is a weekly web content series that I led dedicated to posting literary pieces and art by poets and writers — each piece brewed with emotion, impact, and soul.
Goals of the Segment
  1. Boost Engagement — Literary web content boosted engagements on Facebook by 50%.
  2. Immortalize — To immortalize the medical student experience in words.
Goals (continued)
  1. Outlet for writers and artists — Gave contributors a creative outlet which increased productivity by 75%.
  2. Develop skills — Gained proficiency in Facebook Analytics and Meta Business Suite up to 10 levels.
What worked well — Consistent postings
Maintaining weekly posts helped establish Soulful Sundays as a reliable creative fixture. Audiences began to expect new content every Sunday, building anticipation and routine engagement.
Organic participation & design

Organic participation: Writers contributed freely, producing authentic and diverse voices.

Aesthetic post designs: Visually cohesive layouts and calming palettes enhanced reading and encouraged reflection.

High engagement on emotional pieces
Posts on grief, hope, burnout, or healing received the most reactions and shares — the series sparked meaningful dialogue and deep engagement.

Quick metrics

  • Engagement: +50% on Facebook
  • Productivity: +75% for contributing writers
  • Analytics: improved Meta Business Suite proficiency
